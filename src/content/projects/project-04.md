An interior of strong material identity and controlled contrast, this project is defined by the dialogue between wall veneer surfaces and sculptural stone insertions, establishing a refined yet expressive spatial language. Continuous timber volumes create a cohesive architectural envelope, grounding the space with depth and warmth, while the green marble elements—most prominently the monolithic kitchen island and bathroom vanity—introduce a tactile, almost geological presence.
