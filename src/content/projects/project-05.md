A refined interior defined by depth, continuity, and material intensity, this project builds its identity through the immersive use of dark timber surfaces, creating a strong architectural envelope that unifies all spaces. The richness of the wood is deliberately contrasted by precise insertions of stone—most notably the sculptural green marble island—which acts as a focal point within the open living area. Herringbone flooring extends the spatial flow, while soft, neutral furnishings and controlled lighting temper the overall composition, introducing balance and tactility. The project operates on a clear principle of contrast and restraint, where each element is reduced to its essential expression, resulting in a sophisticated, intimate atmosphere that feels both grounded and timeless.
