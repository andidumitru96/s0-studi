A calm,  interior defined by the interplay of warm walnut joinery and soft mineral surfaces, this project balances tactile richness with architectural restraint. Continuous wood volumes integrate storage and lighting into a unified spatial language, while microcement floors, matte walls, and neutral textiles diffuse light and create a serene backdrop. Carefully placed accents in terracotta and brushed metal introduce depth without disrupting the composition, resulting in a timeless, quietly expressive space where clarity of form, material honesty, and controlled light shape the atmosphere.
